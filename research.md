# Grand Canyon Trip Research

## Wikipedia Summary
Grand Canyon National Park, located in northwestern Arizona, spans 1,217,262 acres and was established as a national monument in 1908 and as a national park in 1919. Designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1979, it attracts nearly 5 million visitors annually. The park protects the iconic Grand Canyon, a deep gorge carved by the Colorado River, and features a range of climate zones from semi‑arid to Mediterranean.
